Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our program

Is this program free?

Yes, Ngao Labs is a community initiative committed to democratizing tech education.

Is it online or physical?

The program is fully virtual, utilizing Google Meet for standups and Discord for collaboration.

What are the pre-requisites?

You need a basic understanding of mathematics and a reliable internet connection. We teach the coding from scratch.

How do I get into the Incubation Programme?

Selection is based on the quality of your final Capstone Project in Week 10.
