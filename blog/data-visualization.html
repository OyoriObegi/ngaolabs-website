Communicating Insights: The Art of Visualization

Don't Just Show Dataâ€”Tell a Story

One of the core modules in Week 3 of our program focuses on Data Visualization. A common mistake junior analysts make is over-complicating their charts.

3 Principles We Teach at Ngao Labs:

  1. Know Your Audience: A technical stakeholder needs different charts than a business executive.
  2. Less is More: Remove "chart junk." If a gridline doesn't add information, delete it.
  3. Color with Purpose: Use color to highlight the insight, not just to make the chart look pretty.

We use libraries like Matplotlib and Seaborn to turn raw numbers into compelling narratives that drive decision-making.
