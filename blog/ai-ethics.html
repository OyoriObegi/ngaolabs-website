AI Safety & Ethics: First Principles

Responsible AI is Not Optional

In the rush to build powerful models, ethics is often an afterthought. At Ngao Labs, we introduce responsible AI concepts in Week 1 and revisit them in Week 9.

We challenge our learners to ask difficult questions:

  • Does this dataset represent the population fairly?
  • Is the model reinforcing existing social biases?
  • How do we protect user privacy?

As we prepare to incubate projects, we ensure that every solution is vetted not just for accuracy (F1 score/RMSE), but for fairness. We are building the next generation of ethical tech leaders.
