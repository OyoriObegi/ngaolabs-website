Keynote Recap: From Data to Impact

How Analytics Powers Intelligent Systems

Recently, Ngao Labs Founder Oyori Obegi had the privilege of delivering a keynote address to university technology students. The topic, "From Data to Impact," focused on moving beyond syntax and code to understanding the real-world value of analytics.

Key Takeaways

1. Context is King
Data without context is just noise. The most successful data analysts are those who understand the business or social problem they are trying to solve, not just the math behind the model.

Oyori Obegi explaining the Ngao Labs mission to university students.

3. Ethical Responsibility
As builders of the future, we carry the weight of ensuring our systems are fair and unbiased. Tech is not neutral; it reflects the data it is fed.
