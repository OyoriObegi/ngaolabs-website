About Ngao Labs

Safeguarding the Next Wave of Tech Leaders through collaborative learning and innovation

Our Mission

To democratize access to data science and technology education by creating inclusive, community-driven learning environments where knowledge is shared peer-to-peer.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has the opportunity to develop data literacy and technical skills, regardless of their background or resources.

Community First

We believe in the power of collaborative learning, where students become teachers and innovation emerges from diverse perspectives working together.
